Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • 100g spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp salt for cooking pasta
  • 65g chopped pancetta
  • 16g grated parmigiano reggiano cheese
  • 16g grated pecorino romano cheese
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, more to taste

Instructions

  1. Put the egg yolks into a bowl, add the grated cheese, pepper, then mix well with a fork and put to one side.
  2. Place pancetta in a pan over low-medium heat. Cook until golden.
  3. Add boiled water to a large pot with the salt. Add pasta and cook per the packet directions.
  4. When the pasta is cooked, add it into the pan and toss to coat in fat.
  5. Add a few tablespoons of pasta water to the eggs and cheese and stir.
  6. Quickly pour in the eggs and cheese. Stir the spaghetti and pancetta so it mixes with the egg mixture, which should thicken but not scramble.
  7. Serve immediately.
